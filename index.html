How to use this dice simulator

Enter a number of die and sides in an input box, then click roll to roll your dice.

You can roll one die by putting in the kind of die you want to roll (like 'd6').

Or, roll multiple dice by putting the nunmber you want to roll in front of the letter d (like 3d20).

Then, your die will roll on screen! And every roll will be stored in your results history.
